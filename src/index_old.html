Brotherland is a visual research about nationalism, racism, violence and broken dreams.


Images, archive material and interviews are used to reintegrate ignored stories into the larger history and bring them back into the collective narrative.


Brotherland is a collaborative project by Martina Zaninelli and Thomas Jacobs.


It's an ongoing work.
